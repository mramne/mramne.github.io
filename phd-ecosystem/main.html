The PhD Ecosystem

This project was born out of frustration with the lack of clarity in roles and resources related to PhD students at Chalmers. Together with colleagues in DrGENIE, a doctoral student organisation working for gender equity at Chalmers, we developed a web application to map and visualize the PhD ecosystem, aiming to enhance understanding and support for PhD students at Chalmers throughout their journey.
